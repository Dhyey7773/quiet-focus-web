Features

Everything you need.
Nothing you don't.

Built for students. Every feature earns its place by making focus easier — not more complicated.

⏱️

Distraction-aware timer

A conic-gradient ring that fills as you focus. When you drift, tap once. The session doesn't end — it adapts. The timer tracks your actual focused minutes, not just elapsed time.

🧠

AI coach check-ins

Short, calm prompts at the right moment. The AI speaks once — not constantly. It never lectures.
